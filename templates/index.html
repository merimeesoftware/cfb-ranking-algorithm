How the Rankings Work (Version 3.4 - Resume-Dominant)

This ranking system balances "Predictive Power" (Elo) with "Deserving Resume" (Record):

Why use Elo?

We use Elo primarily to measure Strength of Schedule. It tells us that a win against Georgia is worth more than a win against Kent State. The Iterative Solver (running the season 3 times) ensures that early-season games are evaluated based on end-of-season team strength.

Final Ranking Formula:
  • Team Quality (50%): The Elo rating. Captures who you played and how you played (margin of victory).
  • Record Score (40%): The raw winning percentage. Captures that you won. This ensures 10-2 teams rank above 8-5 teams.
  • Conference Strength (10%): Contextual tie-breaker.
FRS = (0.5 × TeamQuality) + (0.4 × RecordScore) + (0.1 × ConferenceQuality)
