How the Rankings Work (V3.9)

This ranking system balances Predictive Power (Elo) with Deserving Resume (Record & Schedule Strength), optimized to align with CFP philosophy.

The Formula
FRS = (0.55 × TeamQuality) + (0.35 × RecordScore) + (0.10 × ConfQuality)
  • Team Quality (55%): The Elo rating. Measures pure team strength based on who you played and margin of victory.
  • Record Score (35%): A composite of Winning %, Strength of Schedule (SoS), and Strength of Victory (SoV).
  • Conference Quality (10%): Contextual boost for playing in stronger leagues (weighted by P4 vs P4 performance).
Key Features
  • Logarithmic SoS: Strength of Schedule uses logarithmic scaling for smoother differentiation between tough and elite schedules.
  • Strength of Victory (SoV): Bonus points awarded for defeating opponents with an Elo > 1200.
  • Reduced Legacy Bias: Historical priors blend 30% past performance with 70% fresh tier-based start (configurable). Drops 3-year-old data.
  • Road Warriors: Road wins are weighted 1.1x compared to home wins.
  • Iterative Solver: The season is simulated 3 times to ensure early-season games are evaluated based on end-of-season team strength.
